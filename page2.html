Skyfall

Skyfall is a simple game that I made with a team. In Skyfall, you have three lives and your goal is simple: prevent the asteroids from hitting the ground. You lose a life every time an asteroid hits the ground, and you gain points every time you shoot down an asteroid. When you don't have any lives left, the game ends. It’s a lot more fun than it sounds.

FTL - Into The Void

FTL - Into The Void was the first game I ever made, but it’s still one I’m proud of. It’s a text based game where you play as a chimpanzee handler on humanity’s first attempt at manned FTL flight when things go horribly wrong. The stakes are incredibly high, and there are multiple endings, some good, some … not so good.
