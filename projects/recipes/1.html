My Brownie Recipe

Introduction

I've always loved brownies, and, as such I learned to make them myself. It is significant to me and my family because we have made a tradition of making brownies every other week. Over the years, I've learned a very good brownie recipe and tips to make it work well, and as such I wanted to share my insights. According to my mom, they are The best brownies I've ever tasted!.

Tips and Tricks

  • The timing is not exact: It varies oven by oven, so make sure to test the brownies with your knife a couple minutes before the given time.
  • Take the brownies out when you can stick a knife into the brownies and take it out with only a little bit of brownie sticking to the blade

Ingredients

Total Prep Time: ~15 minutes

Total Cook Time: ~25-35 minutes

Number of Servings: 16

Serving Size: 1 1.5 inch by 1.5 inch brownie

Ingredient Amount
Premade Brownie Mix 1 Box
Eggs 2
Water 1/2 cup
Butter 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Melt the butter in a microwave.
  3. Pour brownie mix into a mixing bowl.
  4. Crack eggs into the mixing bowl, and put the egg shells into the trash.
  5. Pour the water and melted butter into the mixing bowl.
  6. Get an electric eggbeater if available.
  7. If you have the eggbeater, use it to mix the brownie mix until it is all one uniform colour. If you don't have an eggbeater, use whatever other means you prefer to mix the brownie mix until it reaches a consistent colour.
  8. Get out a metal 9 inch by 9 inch tray.
  9. Rub the metal tray with a stick of butter until every bit of the inside of the tray is coated.
  10. Pour the mix into the tray.
  11. Spread the mix across the tray evenly.
  12. When the oven has finished heating, put the mix in the oven for 4 minutes less than the minimum time recommended on the mix box.
  13. Pull brownies out of the oven.
  14. Let brownies cool for 2 minutes.
  15. Cut the partially cooled brownies into sixteenths.
  16. Let brownies cool for a minimum of five additional minutes before serving.
  17. Enjoy!
