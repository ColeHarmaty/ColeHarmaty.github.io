My Brownie Recipe

Introduction

I've always loved brownies, and, as such I learned to make them myself. It is significant to me and my family because we have made a tradition of making brownies every other week. Over the years, I've learned a very good brownie recipe and tips to make it work well, and as such I wanted to share my insights. Acording to my mom, they are the best brownies I've ever tasted!.

Tips and Tricks

  • The timing is not exact: It varies oven by oven, so make sure to test the brownies with your knife a couple minutes before the given time.
