My Salad Recipe

Introduction

Tips and Tricks

  • You can swap out the Thousnad Island salad dressing for one that you like more.
  • If you don't like it, try removing the tomatoes!

Ingredients

Total Prep Time: ~10 minutes

Number of Servings: 1

Serving Size: 1 bowl

Ingredient Amount
Baby Spinach 1 bowl's worth
Full-Size Carrot 1
Thousand Island Salad Dressing ~4 tablespoons
Tomato 1

Instructions

  1. Get a bowl.
  2. Fill the bowl with spinach.
  3. Wash the carrot.
  4. Use a fruit peeler on the carrot over the bowl until you have the amount of carrots that you desire.
  5. Cut up the tomato into thin slices.
  6. Mix the tomato slices into the bowl.
  7. Mix up the salad in the bowl.
  8. Add the salad dressing.
  9. Enjoy!
